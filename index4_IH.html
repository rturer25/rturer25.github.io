2021 Season

Ice Hockey

The Taft Ice Hockey Team went 11-9-2 this season. Some notable games was the 2-1 win over Loomis Chaffee, the 4-1 win over Choate, and the playoff game against Governors Academy. I played left defense and was on the gold powerplay unit.

One of our best games was against Loomis Chaffee. Chloe Agopian and Erin Lonergan each scored a goal putting Taft ahead in the huge upset; beating the undefeated Loomis opponent.

Against the powerful opponent of Choate we rose to the occasion. Erin Lonergan scored twice, Kyla O'Byrne and Chloe Agopian each slotted one to secure the win.

Taft GVH was the only team to make post season play. Despite suffering a brutal defeat against Governors Academy, the Taft community came out to support our squad.
