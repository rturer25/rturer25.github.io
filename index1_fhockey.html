2021 Season

Field Hockey

The Taft Varsity Field Hockey Team went 13-4. We were coached by Ann Bell, Coache Bracco-Mullane, and Coach Danaher. I wear number 22 and play center back.

A big win for us was against Loomis Chaffee. We won 7-0 and center midfielder Maisy Ricciardelli scored a hat trick. Our defense was strong and helped secure the shutout for Jenna Guglielmi.

Another notable win was against Northfield Mount Hermon on our Senior Night. Finley Davignon put Taft on the board first. NMH tied up the game early in the 3rd quarter. Evelyn Hidy scored the game winner with 3 minutes left in the game.

Our biggest win of the season was against our rivals the bearcats on Hotchkiss day. I scored the first goal and Chloe Agopian scored the second in the 2-0 victory. This was the first time Taft field hockey beat Hotchkiss in field hockey for 15 years.
