2021 Season

Field Hockey

The Taft Varsity Field Hockey Team went 13-4. Led by Coach Ann Bell and Assistant Coaches Bracco-Mullane and Danaher. I started every game and played every position on defense, but primarily center back.

One of our notable preformances were the 7-0 victory over Loomis Chaffee. Center midfielder Maisy Ricciardelli scoring a hatrick. Ashley Wright, Captian Callia Ferraris, Evelyn Hidy, and Amelia Lancaster each tallying one goal as well. The Taft defense held strong securing the shutout for senior Captain Jenna Guglielmi.

Another notable win was against Northfield Mount Hermon on Senior Night. Junior Finley Davignon put Taft on the board first. NMH tied up the game early in the 3rd quarter. Evelyn Hidy found the back of the net with 3 minutes left in the game to secure the win.

Our biggest win of the season was against our rivals the bearcats on Hotchkiss day. I scored the first goal and Chloe Agopian scored the second in the 2-0 victory. This was the first time Taft beat Hotchkiss in field hockey for 15 years.
