2022 Season

Track

My events: shotput, discus, and pole vault

I started discus in 8th grade when I was the Captian of my middle school track team. During that season TE Middle School Track won a state championship and I placed 2nd in discus for the state. For highschool my current PR is 105' 8" and I'm shooting to finish my next season over 110" to break the school record. I placed 3rd in the Founders League Championship Meet in this event.

I started pole vault this year to try something new. My current PR is 7'6". I'm hoping to clear 8' by the end of the next season. I placed second in the Founders League Championship Meet at this event.

I started throwing shotput in 8th grade and placed 4th overall at middle school states. My current PR is 28'3" however I will probably be retiring from shotput to try hurdles next season.
